# Time Fantasy style prototype

A **style-direction prototype** for reframing Incanto as a top-down 16-bit JRPG
(Final Fantasy VI / Time Fantasy / "Ancient Kingdoms" era) instead of the current
side-view dark dungeon.

**Open `time_fantasy_prototype.html` in a browser** (or via GitHub Pages) — it
runs with zero downloads.

## What you're looking at

A wizard casting bolts at a skeleton on a grass field — Incanto's own "cast
spells at skeletons" loop, in the new look. The sprites are **original art
generated by the page itself** (see the drawing functions in the HTML), in the
Time Fantasy idiom:

- chibi proportions, a tight shaded palette (2–3 tones per material)
- a crisp 1px dark **auto-outline** — every sprite cell is painted in tones, then
  the silhouette is wrapped in an outline (`outlineCell`). That outline is what
  gives pixel art its clean, "professional pack" read.
- laid out in the standard **RPG Maker VX/MV 3×4 character-sheet format**
  (3 walk/idle frames × 4 facings; the right facing is a mirror of the left).

Because the art is original, there's **no licensing or redistribution problem** —
these sheets are committed to the repo in `generated/`.

## Files

- `time_fantasy_prototype.html` — the runnable prototype + all sprite generation.
- `generated/hero.png`, `generated/skeleton.png` — the exported 48×96 sprite
  sheets (original art). Regenerate any time via the page's `window.__exportSheets()`.
- `assets/` — drop-in slot for a **real** Time Fantasy pack (git-ignored, see below).

## Swapping in a real Time Fantasy pack (optional)

The loader still supports third-party sheets, so you can compare against the real
thing:

1. Grab a pack from the
   [Time Fantasy FREE PACKS collection](https://itch.io/c/310683/time-fantasy-free-packs)
   (or the paid bundle for the full roster).
2. Put a character sheet at `prototype/assets/hero.png` and a monster sheet at
   `prototype/assets/skeleton.png`.
3. Adjust `SHEET_CONFIG` at the top of the HTML (`cellW`/`cellH`, and
   `charCol`/`charRow` to pick a character on a multi-character sheet).
4. Reload. A small green square in the canvas corner confirms real art loaded.

**Why `assets/*.png` is git-ignored:** Time Fantasy's license allows use in games
but forbids redistributing the raw sheets, and this repo is public — so a
purchased/downloaded pack must stay out of git. Our own `generated/` art has no
such restriction.
