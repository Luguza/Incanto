Prototype · placeholder art

Incanto in a Time Fantasy–style top-down JRPG scene

The sprites below are my own placeholder art, drawn in the 16-bit palette and in the exact RPG Maker sheet layout Time Fantasy ships in — 3 walk frames × 4 facings per character. The point is to preview the look and the loader, not the final art.

To see the real thing: download a free Time Fantasy pack, drop hero.png / slime.png into prototype/assets/, and this page renders the true sprites with no code change. Frame sizes are set in SHEET_CONFIG at the top of the script.
