Prototype · original in-house sprites

Incanto in a Time Fantasy–style top-down JRPG scene

The wizard and skeleton are original sprites I generated in the 16-bit Time Fantasy idiom — chibi proportions, a tight shaded palette, and a crisp dark outline. Because they're original, there's no redistribution restriction: these are committable assets, not placeholders.

They're laid out in the standard RPG Maker 3×4 character-sheet format (3 walk frames × 4 facings), so a real Time Fantasy pack can still be dropped into prototype/assets/ to swap them out — set the cell size in SHEET_CONFIG and reload.
